FAQ'S

How to sign up as a new customer?

Customer should register themselves on FoodMania. They can register themselves by few simple clicks. Just click Signup and fill in appropriate information, then login using created id and password and you are good to go.

From where can we login to Customer account?

Customer has to click on Login option on the page to login to their account.

Can user place order if user is not registered on FoodMania?

No, user cannot place order unless registered. it is mandatory to create an account inorder to order online to ensure consistency and integrity.

What steps are taken to ensure Hygiene?

Our Chefs at FoodMania give utmost importance to Healthy, hygienic food service. They not only take complete assurance of the hygiene while preparing your food, but also aim at serving you the best quality food, so that you can enjoy your food without worrying about anything.

How can customer send special request or instruction about food while placing orders?

FoodMania is providing you the facility to send your special request or notes to restaurant with your order. You can write your special notes, recommendations and feedbacks by clicking on the Contact Us option.

Are there offers and discounts available?

Yes, for making customers happy and satisfied, we provide numerous offers. But, the minimum order should be Rs. 400 to avail benefits of those offers.
